Cooked Pepperoni

Description

Something I've been having since I was a young child, while maybe not the most healthy thing in the world it sure is delicious!

It's simple to make, all you need is a microwave, plate, and paper towel. Oh! And of course some pepperoni!

Ingredients

  • Bag of Pepperoni(Either 5oz or 8oz)

Steps

  1. Take plate and line it with several layers of paper towel.
  2. Place pepperoni onto the plate. Spread it out so it is not in one big clump. If you'd like it crispier try to make it a single layer of pepperoni pieces.
  3. Depending on the size of bag you have, I find the 5oz. cooks well at 3 minutes. For the 8 oz. 4 minutes should be good. All microwaves vary though, so this will take some fine tuning to get it excatly how you want it.
