Muenster Cheese Slices

Description

This one is the easiest of the bunch, but the tough part is getting the right block of cheese. It's usually pretty easy, but sometimes it's hit or miss!

Ingredients

  • Block of Muenster Cheese

Steps

  1. This one starts in the store.

    You need to get squeeze a few blocks and get the softest feeling one. I find these have the best texture.

  2. Once you have that perfect block home, slice it up and eat while still chilly. My favorite slice is right between thick and thin!
